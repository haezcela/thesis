[ ADMINISTRATIVE CIRCULAR NO. 49-2001, September 17, 2001 ]








TO


: JUDGES AND COURT PERSONNEL OF THE REGIONAL TRIAL
 COURTS, METROPOLITAN TRIAL COURTS, MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURTS IN CITIES, 
MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURTS, AND MUNICIPAL CIRCUIT TRIAL COURTS






 








SUBJECT


 :OBSERVANCE CORRECTIONAL FROM 21 TO 
CELEBRATION OF THE NATIONAL CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK 27 OCTOBER 2001 AND OF 
THE 14th PRISON AWARENESS SUNDAY ON 28 OCTOBER 2001 BY THE CBCP 
EPISCOPAL COMMISSION ON PRISON PASTORAL CARE








This year's National Correctional Consciousness Week,
 per mandate of Proclamation No. 551 of then President Fidel V. Ramos, 
will be observed from 21 to 27 October 2001.. On 28 October 2001, the 
Episcopal Commission on Prison Pastoral Care (ECPPC) of the Catholic 
Bishops' Conference of the Philippines (CBCP) will celebrate its 14th 
Prison Awareness Sunday (PAS). In line with such celebration, the 
Commission has requested that the Judiciary initiate activities that 
will help ackieve the aims of the National Correctional Consciousness 
Week.


In support of this observance and to promote 
awareness in the Judiciary of the plight of the inmates conformably with
 the mandate of the law, Judges and court personnel of the Regional 
Trial Courts, Metropolitan Trial Courts, Municipal Trial Courts in 
Cities, Municipal Trial Courts, and Municipal Circuit Trial Courts are 
encouraged to visit the provincial, city, or municipal jails and prisons
 within their respective jurisdictions or to hoIcTother related 
activities, and assist in whatever effective and legitimate way the 
celebration of the Prison Awareness Sunday.


The Executive Judges shall schedule the jail 
visitation by the judges and court personnel under their respective 
administrative supervision in a manner that would not disrupt court 
proceedings. Affected parties and their counsel shall be notified in 
advance of any changes in the court calendar by reason of this activity.


The Office of the Court Administrator shall implement this Administrative Circular.


17 September 2001.








(Sgd.) HILARIO G. DAVIDE, JR. 


 








Chief Justice
